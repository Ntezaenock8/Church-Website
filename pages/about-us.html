About Us

Rhema Revival Ministries is a vibrant, Spirit-led community dedicated to spreading the love and truth of Jesus Christ. Founded with a vision to empower believers and transform lives, we are committed to fostering a deep relationship with God through worship, teaching, and fellowship.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by creating an environment where the Holy Spirit moves freely, guiding and inspiring us to live out our faith boldly. We believe in the power of God's Word to revive hearts and renew spirits, as inspired by Romans 8:14: "For all who are led by the Spirit of God are sons of God" [AMPC].

Join us as we worship, serve, and grow together in faith, impacting our community and beyond for the glory of God.
