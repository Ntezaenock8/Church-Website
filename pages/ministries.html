Our Ministries

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is dedicated to empowering young people to grow in faith, develop leadership skills, and build a strong community rooted in Christ. We offer engaging programs, Bible studies, and fun activities to inspire and equip the next generation.

Men's Ministry

The Men's Ministry provides a space for men to connect, grow spiritually, and support one another in their walk with God. Through fellowship, Bible study, and service opportunities, we encourage men to lead with integrity and faith.

Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry fosters a supportive community where women can grow in their relationship with God and each other. We offer Bible studies, prayer groups, and events that inspire women to live out their faith boldly.

Student's Ministry

The Student's Ministry is designed for college and high school students, providing a space to explore faith, ask questions, and build a strong foundation in Christ. We offer mentorship, study groups, and community outreach programs.

Evangelism Ministry

Our Evangelism Ministry is committed to sharing the Gospel with our community and beyond. Through outreach events, mission trips, and personal evangelism, we aim to spread the love and truth of Jesus Christ to all.
